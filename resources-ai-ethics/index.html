Resources on Ethics of AI

In this page I compile a list of resources I found accross the web and found interesting about the topic of Ethics. I will update this list as I find more resources, so come back to check again later! If you find something you think should be added there, a broken link, or an error, feel free to contact me.

Please do not contact me to tell me you’re disagreeing or you don’t like one or the other source. I put in here source I found interesting, with very different styles and tone, some I fully agree with, others I don’t really but helped me in my thinking-process somehow.
