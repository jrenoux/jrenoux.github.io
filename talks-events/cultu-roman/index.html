Cultu-RO-MAN

I was part of the organizing committe of the Ro-MAN workshop on Cultural Influences in Human-Robot Interaction: Today and Tomorrow

Jennifer Renoux
Aug 30, 2022 Worksohp
Image credit: Ro-Man 2022

On August 25, 2022, we hosted the Cultu-ro workshsop at Ro-MAN 2022. The workshop will focused on the cultural influences in human-robot interaction. Robots have the potential to improve services in areas such as eldercare, rehabilitation, training, and education.

More information and detail of the program available on the workshop website
